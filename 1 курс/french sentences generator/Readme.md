generator.py
Generates five random French sentences, each of a different type. 

nomsfem.txt, nomsmasc.txt – feminine and masculine nouns
pronomspers.txt – personal pronouns and indefinite pronoun 'on'
verbsItransit.txt, verbsIItransit.txt - transitive verbs from groups I and II (without -er and -ir verbs)
verbsIintransr.txt, verbsIIintrans.txt - intransitive verbs from groups I and II (without -er and -ir verbs)
adj.txt – masculine adjectives
